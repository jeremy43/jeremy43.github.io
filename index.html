Hi! I’m Yuqing Zhu / 祝于晴
A research scientist at TikTok and I received my computer science PhD from UC Santa Barbara, fortunately advised by Prof.Yu-Xiang Wang. Prior to that, I received my B.S. in computer science from Nanjing University, fortunately advised by Prof. Wu-Jun LI.
Research Interests
My research interest is machine learning, including differential privacy, domain adaptation and federated learning. Recently, I am focusing on how to establish rigorous diﬀerential privacy guarantees for large scale real dataset. I am also working on an open source project Auto DP , which is an automating diﬀerential privacy computation, to help researchers obtain provable DP guarantees with advanced techniques in differential privacy.
